SRD CICD, product Owner

The Space Range and Display suite of application is live in all twelve countries that Tesco operated in. There are three different major versions throughout the countries and additionally each country has individual customisations. This makes maintaining and upgrading very difficult and time consuming for the team, as well as frustrating for the customer who use outdated software.

The focus of this project is to improve the engineering platform for SRD to a single code base across all countries and the ability to automatically deploy new code everywhere reliably.

As a Product Owner I worked closely with the Solution Architect and the Tech Expert to refine what was needed to achieve this goal and the reasons why it is important to do it in such a way. Then I broke down the larger tasks into manageable user stories to add to the backlog so that the development team could quickly progress from one story to the other.
